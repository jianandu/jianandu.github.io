## Research Agenda

My research examines how **premodern beliefs**—particularly religious and supernatural thinking—continue to shape **bureaucratic behaviors** in modern authoritarian states.

### Questions I Ask

1. **How do premodern beliefs persist?** Tracing the transmission of religious and supernatural ideas from imperial China to the modern bureaucratic apparatus.

2. **How do beliefs affect policy?** Examining how officials' private beliefs—about Feng Shui, lucky dates, fortune-telling—influence concrete policy outcomes.

3. **What are the institutional consequences?** Understanding how informal beliefs interact with formal institutions to shape governance.
